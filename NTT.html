Nursery Teacher Training (NTT) Admission, Eligibility & Duration

Nursery Teacher Training NTT Course is a diploma course for those who want to teach & cater to the Nursery Stage or Pre Primary level students. After Completed these course candidates are trained in the field like Child Education, Teaching Methodology, Nursery Level, Preschool Subjects & Communication.

Rural-Urban Council of Skills & Vocational Studies is one of the best NTT Course Training Provider Institution in India. We have more than 200 NTT Course Training & Learning Centers all over India.

  • Duration of NTT Course: 1 & 2 Year Course
  • Type of NTT Course: Diploma

Document Required for NTT Admission:

  • 10th, 12th Certificate Photocopy.
  • 5 Passport size photographs.
  • Aadhaar Card Photocopy with self-attested.
  • Completely Filled Admission Form.
  • Eligibility for NTT Admission:

Min 12th pass candidate can apply for NTT Admission.

Career Option after course:

  • Job opportunities in Pre School after Nursery Teacher Training Course.
  • Job opportunities in Private & Govt. School as a Nursery Teacher after Nursery Teacher Training Course.
  • Job opportunities in Contract based Teacher Job after Nursery Teacher Training Course.
  • Start your own Daycare Preschool & Nursery school after Nursery Teacher Training Course.
  • Start Your Institution after Nursery Teacher Training Course.
  • Start Social Work in Childhood Care sector after Nursery Teacher Training Course.
  • Start your own coaching Center after Nursery Teacher Training Course.
  • Age Criteria for Nursery Teacher Training Course.
  • There is no upper age limit for enrolling in an NTT course.
